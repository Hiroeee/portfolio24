Project Overview

Franco Manca is a pioneering sourdough pizza in the UK and was named the UK's best Italian Restaurant on Yelp. Sadly, most users commented their app "never works" or "it's useless".The project goal is to identify usability issues and enhance user engagement and satisfaction for the Franco Manca app.

Develop
SCAMPER:

At this stage, creative brainstorming plays a critical role. I applied the SCAMPER method that can improve or innovate products that are not performing well in the market. It takes seven different ways to reimagine a product. I came up with solutions to ease the severe problems:.

1Safer registration experience:
Require only essential user details during the registration process. Also, supply alternative socials login for a choice.

2Better issue handling experience:
Improving the error copy for the virtual royalty card that includes "contacting us" as one solution.

3Merge the features:
Combining order & collect and the menu supplies a smoother effortless order experience. Also, stick an order through the app instead of jumping onto the company. Simplify the contents for a better user experience.

4Enhance the UI design for better accessibility:
Typeface, typo, colour, touch targets, microcopy.

5Clear navigation:
Replace the complex hamburger and bottom menu bars with a concise bottom navigation bar.

Revised Information architecture:

The original information architecture is convoluted that users struggle to find the contents they want immediately. I clarified the IA with fewer menu contents that we should robustly focus on business tracking as a priority.

Wireframes:

First, I sketched out possible solutions on paper for the main screens to generate different prototype layouts rather than focusing on design details. Then, I moved to commit to mid and high-fidelity designs for further development.

Conclusion
Responsive Design:

It was my first redesign project and a fun problem to tackle. My main challenges were enhancing the usability and accessibility of the app to improve customer satisfaction without violating the brand image. Also, I attempted the subtle content evolution compared to the original design that minimises user alienation for the existing users.
